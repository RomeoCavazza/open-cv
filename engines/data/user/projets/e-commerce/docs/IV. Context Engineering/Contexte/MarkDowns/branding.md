## Brand Name

### DRYVIA

Strategic rationale:

- **Dry** = Core benefit (immediate understanding)
- **Via** = Movement, journey, performance
- Short, memorable, international, premium
- Coherent with fitness / tech / eco positioning

## Brand Identity

**Vibe:** Nike + Apple Fitness + Modern Ecology

**Positioning:** Performance — Hygiene — Eco — Indoor Training

**Tone:** Direct, motivating, clean, scientific, reassuring

## Verbal Identity

**Main Tagline:** "Stay Dry. Train Hard."

**Alternative Taglines:**

- "Your indoor performance, finally dry."
- "Clean training starts at your feet."
- "Designed for sweat. Engineered for dry."

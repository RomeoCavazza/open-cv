**ITONICS, at the heart of innovation**

ITONICS is the world’s leading Innovation Operating System.

Our AI-powered SaaS platform helps large companies identify emerging

technologies, trends, and market opportunities to shape their innovation strategies.

With more than 150 innovation rockstars across Paris, Berlin, Nuremberg, Cape

Town, Kathmandu, and Miami, we support global leaders such as Adidas, Johnson &

Johnson, Toyota, Siemens, Mondelez, and KPMG.

**Your mission**

Join our Data & AI team to develop artificial intelligence solutions integrated into

our platform.

You will work on the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs), the design of

Python microservices, and the implementation of modern data architectures.

**⚙️ Your daily responsibilities**

● Develop Python microservices for data processing and enrichment.

● Design and consume APIs to interact with AI models.

● Experiment with LangChain and conversational agents.

● Deploy and maintain environments using Docker.

● Contribute to building data pipelines and setting up vector databases.

● Explore new approaches in agentic AI and LLM interaction.

**Required profile (Must-have)**

● Student in computer science, data science, or AI.

● Strong skills in Python, Docker, and API design.

● Curiosity about Large Language Models (LLMs) and modern data

architectures.

● Interest in research, experimentation, and innovation.

Nice to have

● Experience with LangChain, vector databases, datalakes

● Knowledge of prompt engineering or agentic AI

● Understanding of data pipelines and cloud deployment
